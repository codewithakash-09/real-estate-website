Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are needed for buying a property?

You'll need Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, passport-size photos, income proof for loan, and bank statements. We assist with complete documentation.

Can I get a home loan for GDA flats?

Yes! All major banks provide home loans for GDA flats at competitive rates. We have tie-ups with HDFC, ICICI, Piramal, and Indian Bank.

What is the processing time for property registration?

Registration typically takes 2-3 weeks after document verification. Our team handles the complete process including stamp duty and registration.

Do you deal in resale properties?

Yes, we deal in both new and resale properties. Our resale inventory includes GDA flats, builder apartments, and independent houses.
